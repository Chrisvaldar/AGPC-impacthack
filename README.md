# Yo-Chi x AGPC

**Monash ImpactHack x AGPC — Path 1: Commercial Partnership**

---

## What is this?

A partnership concept pitching Yo-Chi (Australian frozen yogurt brand) as an official dessert partner of the Australian Grand Prix Corporation, spanning both F1 (Melbourne) and MotoGP (Phillip Island).

---

## The idea

Yo-Chi is one of the fastest-growing brands with Gen Z in Australia, but has never activated at the country's biggest live sporting weekend. Its self-serve, build-it-fast format is a natural fit for "pit stop" branding.

The concept runs as a 12-month cycle rather than a single race-week stunt:
- **Countdown month** — guess-the-winner promotions, merch-discount days, a limited race-week cup design
- **Event days** — an on-site pop-up in Pitstop Park with sampling
- **Post-race** — a flavour named after the winning team's colour, launched within days
- **Off-season** — loyalty points carry over into MotoGP six months later, then combine into a single lottery for race tickets and a paddock experience
